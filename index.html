About Real Madrid

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. Founded on 6 March 1902, the club has traditionally worn a white home kit since inception. Madrid is one of the most widely supported teams worldwide and is the most successful club in European football history.
